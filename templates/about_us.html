So you want to get a job?

You asked and we answered! OK, maybe you didn't ask, but, we want to get jobs too...

Our team decided to put our data skills to the test!

We built an interactive website that is useful to bootcampers looking for a job in Data Science/Analytics. Our data source begins with a collection of tables from the ETL Project, consisting of five layers: ds_jobs, salary, breweries, and state_boundary. All records have a state ID. Here we propose to build a dynamic front-end for job seekers to map and make figures that will be useful when searching for a job. Some records (such as brewery locations) also have coordinates to map their locations within the states.
